Common Questions about Consulting

What does consulting cost?

An initial conversation to get to know each other is always free and non-binding with us. The costs for further consultations, strategy workshops, or long-term support are calculated individually based on your needs and the project scope.

How does the initial consultation work?

Relaxed and targeted. We meet online (Zoom/Teams) or talk on the phone. You tell me where the shoe pinches, and I give a first assessment.

Online or on-site?

We work primarily digitally, but are very happy to come to your company personally for workshops or kick-offs in the Stuttgart/Heilbronn area.

What do I get after the consultation?

Depending on the package, you will receive a written strategy paper, audit protocols, editorial plan templates, or recordings of our workshops.

Can I continue on my own afterwards?

Absolutely. Our goal is "help for self-help". We give you the tools to successfully manage your channel yourself in the long term.

What if I want full service after all?

No problem. If you notice that you lack the time for implementation, we can seamlessly switch to our full-service support.
