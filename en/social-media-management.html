Common Questions about Social Media Management

What is included in management?

We take over the operational administration of your account. This means: We ensure regular publication of your posts and take care of community management. We answer comments and messages directly as far as possible, and reliably forward specific inquiries to you. Extensive content production like video editing or graphic design are separate modules.

How often do you post?

That depends on your chosen package and goals. Typically, we start with 2-3 high-quality posts per week to build a consistent presence without neglecting quality.

Do I have to provide content?

Ideally, we work with material we create during a joint content shoot. If you already have good photos/videos, we are happy to include them. We can also create content completely independently.

Can I continue posting myself?

Of course! It's your channel. We take care of the planned base work, but spontaneous insights, stories from daily life, or personal posts from you are often the perfect addition. We simply coordinate briefly so everything fits together.

How long is the minimum term?

We believe in results, not restrictive contracts. Typically, we work with a minimum term of 3 months to build the channel sustainably. After that, the collaboration can be canceled monthly.

What happens to existing content?

We conduct an audit of your previous content at the beginning. We keep what worked well; we optimize what didn't fit or start fresh with a new strategy.
