Instagram "Boost Post" is not advertising

The blue "Boost Post" button on Instagram is a marketing trick by Meta. You pay for reach – but without real targeting, without conversion tracking, without optimization. The money evaporates because you can't control who sees your ad or how it performs. It's like throwing banknotes out the window and hoping the right customer walks by.

🎯

Meta Ads Manager: The Professional Tool

Meta Ads Manager is the professional tool behind the scenes. Here you can granularly define who sees your ad – by interests, behavior, demographics, custom audiences from your website, or customer list. You can run A/B tests, test different creatives, automatically distribute budgets to the best ads, and measure exactly which ad leads to which result. That is the difference between hope and strategy.

📊

Why a pro should handle it

Ads Manager is powerful – but also complex. Wrong settings can burn your budget in hours. Without experience, you don't know which metrics really count, when to scale, and when to stop a campaign. We have managed over €10,000 in ad budget and achieved results like 8 cents per link click and CPMs of €2-3 – values far below the industry average.

🚀 How we help you

We take over the complete campaign strategy, setup in Ads Manager, creative creation, and continuous optimization. You see transparent reports and real results – no guesswork, no wasted budgets. Professional performance marketing that drives your business forward.

Our Process

01

Analysis & Strategy

We jointly define your goals (leads, sales, traffic) and analyze your target audience. Based on this, we develop a tailored campaign strategy.

02

Creative & Setup

Our designers create high-converting ad creatives. In parallel, we set up technical tracking (Pixel/CAPI) so we can measure every success.

03

Launch & Test

We launch the campaigns. In the first phase, we test different target groups and ads against each other to identify the winners.

04

Optimization & Scaling

We turn off unprofitable ads and distribute the budget to the winners. Once the ROI is right, we scale the budget for more revenue.

Common Questions about Meta Ads

How much budget do I need?

To provide the algorithm with enough data, we recommend a starting budget of at least €500 - €1,500 per month (plus service fee). Below that, the learning phase often takes too long.

How fast will I see results?

You see the first clicks and traffic immediately after launch. For stable conversions and optimized costs, the algorithm usually needs 2–4 weeks of learning time.

What does the service cost?

Our service fee depends on the advertising budget and complexity. It usually starts at a fixed monthly amount that covers setup, creatives, and optimization.

Do I need a Business Manager?

Yes, a Business Manager and an ad account are necessary. If you don't have one yet, we will handle the complete setup for you.

Who creates the ads?

We do that. We write, design, and edit your ads, as the creative accounts for 70-80% of success today. Your input (images/videos) is, of course, welcome.

How often is it optimized?

We monitor your campaigns daily. We conduct in-depth optimizations and tests weekly to give the algorithm time to learn.
