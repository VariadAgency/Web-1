Common Questions about Content Production

What does a shoot cost?

The cost of a shoot depends individually on the effort and scope. For larger projects or regular content, we offer attractive package prices or retainer models. Let's talk about your needs!

How long does production take?

A simple shoot is often done in half a day. Post-production takes 2–5 working days depending on the effort. We define clear timelines in advance.

What equipment do you use?

We rely on modern equipment from Sony (Alpha series), DJI (drones & gimbals), and professional lighting technology from Aputure to achieve cinematic results.

In what formats do you deliver?

You receive all files optimized for your purpose: High-resolution for print/web as well as compressed and in the correct aspect ratio (e.g., 9:16) for social media.

Do you come to us?

Yes, we are mobile and work throughout the Stuttgart, Heilbronn, and Ludwigsburg region directly at your location or at a location of your choice.

Who owns the usage rights?

You receive comprehensive usage rights for the agreed channels. A complete buy-out (unlimited in time and space) is also possible.
